An all-day workshop for 50+ faculty and students introducing core machine learning concepts from the perspective of those trained in causal inference. Includes a hands-on exercise.

University of Chicago, Chicago, IL — 2024

Two-hour long workshop for Harris students that focuses on translating skills in Python and R into work in MATLAB. Participants leave the session with a viable code sample in MATLAB suitable to share with employers.
